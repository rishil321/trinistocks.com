About This Website

This site is being developed by Rishi Latchmepersad to help fellow Trinbagonian investors understand the Trinidad and Tobago Stock Exchange (TTSE) a bit better. I initially started this site just to develop my software development skills, but I've since realised its value in making charts/tables for TTSE data easily accessible to the public.
